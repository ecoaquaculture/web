Features

1.Suitable used for pre-solid-liquid separation processing of aquaculture raw water treatment.

2.Suitable used for pre-solid-liquid separation processing of aquaculture industrial water treatment.

3.Widely used in various lines which required pre-solid-liquid separation. Such as urban
