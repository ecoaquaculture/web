MBBR biofilm carrier can be used in both aerobic process and anaerobic process.
MBBR is a cost-effective solution for existing activated sludge plants that need more advanced treatment.
Because little or no additional tankage is required, it is an ideal solution for plants with limited space for expansion.
It is also an excellent choice for space efficient, high performance new plant designs.
